This article is a reflection — a calling — inspired by tragedies that unfolded between January and May 2025. It’s for every devotee who set out in search of peace and found pain instead.

Sree Lairai Devi Temple Stampede – Goa, India

Date: May 3, 2025

During the annual Lairai Zatra festival, chaos erupted in the narrow lanes of Shirgao as thousands surged toward the temple. Six lives were lost, and over 60 were injured in the stampede.

Source: The Tribune, The Times of India

Kumbh Mela Stampede – Prayagraj, India

Date: January 29, 2025

A sacred immersion turned tragic as at least 30 pilgrims died and 60 were injured in a stampede at the Triveni Sangam.

Source: AP News, The Guardian
